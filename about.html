I hold a certification in Cognitive and Behavioral Therapy (CBT), completed over three years of training with the French Association for Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (AFTCC). Clinical experience in France and abroadExpérience clinique en France et à l'étranger

I have over 10 years of experience working in both public and private health sectors, supporting individuals across the lifespan and facing a wide range of psychological challenges.Contenu disponible en anglais — version française à venir.

At the Hospital of Versailles (Department of Psychiatry), I conducted comprehensive assessments for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes, working with individuals experiencing severe and chronic psychiatric disorders such as psychotic disorders, mood disorders, and personality disorders. My approach emphasized collaboration with patients, families, general practitioners, social workers, and patient associations to ensure holistic and personalized care.Contenu disponible en anglais — version française à venir.

I also worked extensively with children and adolescents with developmental disorders, designing interventions to strengthen executive functioning, autonomy, and social skills. These interventions took place in multiple settings — at home, in schools, and in workplaces — to promote integration and long-term growth.Contenu disponible en anglais — version française à venir.

Alongside my public sector work, I have provided individual therapy in private practice, supporting clients of all ages dealing with anxiety, depression, grief, relational difficulties, emotional regulation, low self-esteem, and identity issues.Contenu disponible en anglais — version française à venir.
